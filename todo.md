# UA-CMS: Editorial Desk Handover

## Current Status
The **Refined Editorial Pipeline** is fully implemented and ready for pre-testing.

### What's Live:
- **Two-Stage Generation**:
    1. **Gemini 2.0 Flash (Research)**: Google Search grounding enabled to gather "Signal".
    2. **Claude 3.5 Sonnet (Synthesis)**: High-density single-pass generation of the Substack Article, Article Spine, and 5 Narrative Text Posts.
- **Narrative Arc**: The 5 text posts collectively lead to the Substack article and are stored with sequential IDs (`UA-POST-yyyymmdd-nn`).
- **Linguistic Constraints**: No em-dashes, no commas after "and/but/or", and British English enforced across all outputs.
- **Downstream Video**: Video scripts are generated by Gemini *after* the synthesis, explicitly reinforcing the Claude-written article.
- **Kanban UI**: Strategic Research and Narrative Text Posts are visible in the Inquiry detail view.
